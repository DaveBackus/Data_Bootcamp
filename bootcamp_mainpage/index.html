Before the first class

  • Make sure you have a laptop computer to use in class. This is a must.
  • Take our entry poll (click on the link, follow instructions).
  • Join a discussion group: UG | MBA.
  • Bring a nameplate to class. If you don’t have one, use this template.
  • Non-Stern students only: activate your Stern account to get wireless access. If you have trouble, stop by the Help Desk in the lobby between KMC and Tisch or email helpdesk@stern.nyu.edu.
